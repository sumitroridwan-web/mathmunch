Page 2 · One Balloon
Benny the Bear woke up and found a beautiful red balloon floating in the air. He counted it carefully. One! Just one big, round, shiny balloon!
I have ONE balloon. Let me count — one! That's 1!
💡 Number 1 — One!

1 means just one thing. Point and say: one! One balloon, one bear, one sunny day!

Page 3
Two balloons! One red, one yellow!
Page 3 · Two Balloons
The next day, Benny found TWO balloons! A red one and a yellow one. He counted them slowly: one… two! Two is one more than one.
One, two! I have TWO balloons now. Two is more than one!
💡 Number 2 — Two!

2 means two things. Count with your fingers: one, two! Now you have TWO!

2
🤔 Think About It!

Benny has two balloons. Count them — how many?

Page 4
Three balloons! 1, 2, 3!
Page 4 · Three Balloons
Benny found THREE balloons — red, yellow, and green. He counted: one, two, three! Three is one more than two. Benny did a little happy dance!
One, two, THREE! I can count to three! Watch me!
💡 Number 3 — Three!

Count the balloons: 1, 2, 3. Three fingers up!

3
🤔 Think About It!

Count the balloons — how many are there?

Page 5
Four balloons! 1, 2, 3, 4!
Page 5 · Four Balloons
Now Benny had FOUR balloons! He needed two paws to hold them all. Red, yellow, green, and purple — four colours, four balloons!
One, two, three, FOUR! Four balloons for me!
💡 Number 4 — Four!

Four fingers! 1, 2, 3, 4. One more than three!

4
🤔 Think About It!

Benny has four balloons. Which number is four?

Five balloons! Happy Birthday!
Page 6 · Five Balloons — The Birthday Party!
On his birthday, Benny had FIVE beautiful balloons! He counted every single one: one, two, three, four, FIVE! Everyone clapped and cheered!
One, two, three, four, FIVE! Five is my favourite number today!
💡 Number 5 — Five!

Hold up five fingers! 1, 2, 3, 4, 5. You can count to FIVE!
