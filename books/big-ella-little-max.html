Page 2
Ella is big! Max is small!
Page 2 · Big and Small
Meet Ella the Elephant and Max the Mouse. Ella is very big — tall and wide and round! Max is very small — tiny and quick.

Together they are the best of friends!
I am big! said Ella. And I am small! said Max. Both are just right!
💡 Big and Small

Big means large, wide, or heavy. Small means tiny, thin, or light. We use these words to compare things!

BIG
large · wide · huge
small
tiny · little · mini
🤔 Think About It!

Who is BIGGER — Ella or Max?

Page 3
Tall things go up, up, up! Short things stay low!
Page 3 · Tall and Short
Ella stood next to a tall tree and Max stood next to a short flower.

"The tree is tall!" said Ella, stretching up.

"The flower is short!" said Max, looking down.
Tall things go up, up, up! Short things stay down low!
💡 Tall and Short

Tall means going high up. Short means not very high. We compare heights by standing things next to each other!

🤔 Think About It!

Which word means NOT tall?

Page 4
Ella's trunk is long! Max's tail is short!
Page 4 · Long and Short
Ella had a very long trunk that could reach all the way to the treetops!

Max had a short curly tail.

"My trunk is long!" said Ella. "My tail is short!" squeaked Max.
Long things go far away! Short things stay close!
💡 Long and Short

Long means stretching far. Short means not very far. You can measure length with your hands!

🙌 Use your hands! Stretch your arms wide — that's LONG. Put your hands close — that's SHORT!
🤔 Think About It!

Which is longer — Ella's trunk or Max's tail?

Page 5
Ella is heavy! Max is light!
Page 5 · Heavy and Light
Ella and Max sat on a seesaw. Ella's side went all the way down because she was so heavy. Max's side went all the way up because he was so light.

"I am heavy!" laughed Ella. "I am light as a feather!" squeaked Max.
Heavy things go down. Light things go up. Feel the difference!
💡 Heavy and Light

Heavy things are hard to lift. Light things are easy to lift. You can feel the difference by picking things up!

🤔 Think About It!

Ella is heavy and Max is light. On a balance scale, which side goes DOWN?

Page 6
We can compare everything around us!
Page 6 · Compare!
Now Ella and Max knew all the comparison words: big and small, tall and short, long and short, heavy and light.

They looked around and could compare everything they saw!
Comparing things helps us describe the world! I'm big and you're small — but we're BOTH special!
BIG ↔ small
TALL ↔ short
LONG ↔ short
HEAVY ↔ light
🤔 Think About It!

A rock and a feather — which is heavier?

Page 7
Great job — you can compare sizes! 🎉
Page 7 · The End
Ella and Max had so much fun learning together!

"We compared big and small, tall and short, long and short, and heavy and light!" said Max.

"And now YOU can compare things too!" said Ella with a smile. "Look around — what can you see that is big? What is small?"
You did it! You can compare sizes just like us!
