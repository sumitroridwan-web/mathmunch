Page 3 · The Circle Kingdom
Rosie meets Sir Circle. He is perfectly round.

"I have no sides and no corners!" he says.

Wheels, coins and clocks are all circles!

Page 4 · The Triangle Tower
Rosie meets Lady Triangle. She loves her three corners!

"Count my sides — one, two, three!"

Triangles look like mountains!
🔺 Triangle Facts

A TRIANGLE has 3 SIDES and 3 CORNERS.
It looks like a mountain or a slice of pizza!

Page 5
All 4 sides are equal!
Page 5 · The Square Palace
Rosie meets Duke Square. He has 4 equal sides!

"All my sides are the same length!" he says proudly.

Windows and tiles are often squares.
🟥 Square Facts

A SQUARE has 4 SIDES and 4 CORNERS.
All sides are the same length! That makes it special!

🤔 Think About It!

A square has 4 sides. A triangle has 3 sides. Which has more sides?

Page 6 · The Rectangle Room
Rosie finds the Rectangle Room. It looks like a stretched square!

"Two sides are long and two sides are short," says Rosie.

Books and doors are rectangles!
📐 Rectangle Facts

A RECTANGLE has 4 sides and 4 corners.
Two sides are long. Two sides are short.

🤔 Think About It!

Which shape has 4 corners?
