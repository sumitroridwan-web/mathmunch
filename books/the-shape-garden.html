A circle is perfectly round — no corners!
Page 2 · The Circle
Maya walked into her magical shape garden. The first thing she saw was the bright yellow sun shining in the sky.

'A circle!' she said. 'Round and round with no corners at all!'
Look at the sun! It's a perfect circle — round and round with no pointy bits!
💡 What is a Circle?

A circle is perfectly round. It has no corners and no straight sides. Draw one in the air with your finger!

Page 3
A square has 4 equal sides and 4 corners!
Page 3 · The Square
Maya found a square house in the garden. It had four sides, all the same length, and four corners.

'Every side is the same!' said Maya. 'That makes it a square!'
One side, two sides, three sides, four! And look — all the same! It's a square!
💡 What is a Square?

A square has 4 sides of the same length and 4 corners. Count the corners on the house!

Page 4
A triangle has 3 sides and 3 corners!
Page 4 · The Triangle
Next, Maya saw triangle-shaped mountains. 'They look like pizza slices!' she laughed.

Each mountain had three sides and three pointy corners at the top. She counted: one corner, two corners, three!
Triangles everywhere! Up — that's one corner! Up — two! And the pointy top — THREE corners!
💡 What is a Triangle?

A triangle has 3 sides and 3 corners. The corners can be big or small, pointy or flat!

🤔 Think About It!

How many corners does a triangle have?

Page 5
A rectangle has 4 sides — but 2 pairs of equal sides!
Page 5 · The Rectangle
Maya opened the tall rectangle gate to the secret garden.

'A rectangle has four sides like a square,' she said, 'but two sides are long and two sides are short!'
Long, short, long, short — that's four sides! But rectangles are not squares because the sides aren't all the same length!
💡 What is a Rectangle?

A rectangle has 4 sides and 4 corners. Two long sides and two short sides. A square is a SPECIAL rectangle where all sides are equal!

🤔 Think About It!

Which shape has 2 long sides and 2 short sides?

Page 7
Maya knows all the shapes!
Page 7 · Well Done!
Maya skipped happily through her shape garden.

She had found them all — the round circle, the equal-sided square, the three-cornered triangle, and the long-and-short rectangle!

'Shapes are everywhere!' she sang. 'I can name them all!'
Circle, square, triangle, rectangle — shapes are everywhere and I know them ALL!
