✅ Even Numbers

EVEN: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10...
Even numbers always end in 0, 2, 4, 6, or 8!
They can always be split into two equal groups.

⚡ Odd Numbers

ODD: 1, 3, 5, 7, 9...
Odd numbers always end in 1, 3, 5, 7, or 9!

🔑 The Quick Trick!

Look at the LAST digit of any number!
Ends in 0, 2, 4, 6, 8 → EVEN
Ends in 1, 3, 5, 7, 9 → ODD
