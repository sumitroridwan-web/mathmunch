MathMunch is designed to support teachers, not replace them. Assign specific games, track class progress, and align every activity to your Cambridge Primary scheme of work — all from one simple dashboard.

  • ✅ Curriculum-mapped to Cambridge Primary Mathematics (Stages 1–6)
  • ✅ Real-time class progress dashboard with strand breakdown
  • ✅ Assign games to individual students or whole groups
  • ✅ Printable progress reports and certificates
  • ✅ No student logins required for Early Years learners
  • ✅ Works on tablets, laptops, and interactive whiteboards
